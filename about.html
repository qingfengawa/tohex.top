The Birth of TOHEX

2023年5月20日晚上10点，KARPED1EM发布了Town Of Host Edited Ver. 2.3.6并宣称TOHE将不再受到支持。
此后的5天内，KARPED1EM陆续关闭EAC Cloud Service、车队姬服务、TOHE官网。
TOHE在她最辉煌的时刻，离开了Among Us。

在所有Au玩家为TOHE的离开而惋惜之时，TOHEX——诞生了。

No way to know

Just as TOHEX was thriving, their large group was suddenly banned.
Who is the initiator behind this? They have no way of knowing.
Although the so-called insider reported to the TOHEX development team that KARPED1EM had done it, they did not adopt this "answer"
This is a very wise choice. This prevents them from paying a completely unnecessary price for it.
